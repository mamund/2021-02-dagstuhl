The Maze Ontology

Here, we present the terms of the Maze ontology for my RDFisation of Mike Amundsen's Maze API. Can also be read using your favourite RDFa reader.

Terms

A Maze is something to get lost in. A Maze is comprised of Cells.
It may be that you can leave a cell through a door in different Directions:
  • north
  • east
  • south
  • west
